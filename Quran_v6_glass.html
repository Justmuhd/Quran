"And We send down of the Quran that which is healing and mercy for the believers." — 17:82

The Quran is the final and complete revelation from Allah to humanity, sent through the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ over 23 years. It addresses every dimension of human existence — faith, law, ethics, history, science, and the soul. Over 1.8 billion Muslims recite it daily, millions have memorised it completely, and it has remained unchanged for over 1,400 years — not a single letter altered. It is the most read, most memorised, and most studied book in all of human history. To engage with the Quran is to engage with the divine — may it be a light, a healing, and a guide for all who seek it.
